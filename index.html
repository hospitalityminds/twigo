About Us

Featuring free WiFi and a restaurant, Hotel Twigo inn offers accommodation in Mumbai. The property is located in Andheri. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Every room at this hotel is air-conditioned and is fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Certain rooms have a seating area for your convenience. You will find a kettle in the room. The rooms are fitted with a private bathroom. For your comfort, you will find free toiletries and a hair dryer. The property provides 24-hour concierge desk and room service.
